We had such a great time it was a blast! I tried to help my friend Tien learn how to surf and I did my best to break down the steps for him. He managed to get up on his knees and ride the wave. It was super cool to see how excited his success made him. I told him to do this:

  1. paddle out
  2. learn to dive under the waves or go over them
  3. sit on your board and look for a wave
  4. when you see a wave start paddling towards shore (really hard)
  5. when you feel the wave pushing you and feel that you don't need to paddle anymore pop up
